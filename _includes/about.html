About

I am (almost) an Informatics Engineer from Universidad Catolica Andres Bello, located in Caracas, Venezuela. My main dream and goal in life is to pursue a career in robotics research, in the fields of optimal control theory and computer vision.

For my (Bachellor) thesis I worked on the development of the electronics, state estimation, wireless communication, ROS-based ground station, and a PID control algorithm to stabilize and control a low-cost quadrotor platform.

In my free time I enjoy running, playing soccer, studying mathematics, playing guitar, hanging out with friends, and reading a lot.
